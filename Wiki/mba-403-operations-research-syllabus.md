# M.B.A. 403: Operations Research Syllabus

**Summary**: The syllabus for the M.B.A. course 403 on Operations Research, covering various quantitative techniques for management.

**Sources**: operations research - Sunitha.jpg

**Last updated**: 2026-04-13

---

This wiki focuses on Unit I of the syllabus, which introduces the foundations of Operations Research and Linear Programming.

## Unit I Outline
- Introduction to OR: Meaning and scope.
- Linear Programming Problem (LPP): Definition and formulation.
- Solving LPP: Graphical method and Simplex algorithm (including the Fundamental theorem of LPP).
- Advanced Simplex: Concept of artificial variables, Big-M method, and Two-phase simplex method.
- Duality: Concept of duality and Dual simplex method.
